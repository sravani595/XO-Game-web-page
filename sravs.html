XO Game

XO Game Description

Setup:

  1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  2. One player uses the mark "X" and the computer uses the mark "O".
How to Play:
  1. Taking Turns:
    • Players alternate turns. On your turn, place your mark ("X" or "O") in any empty cell of the grid.
  2. Winning the Game:
    • The first player to get three of their marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, wins the game.
    • After a player wins, the game ends immediately.
  3. Draw:
    • If all cells are filled and no player has achieved three marks in a row, the game ends in a draw.
Winning Combinations:
  • Horizontal: Three marks in a row on any of the three horizontal lines.
  • Vertical: Three marks in a row on any of the three vertical lines.
  • Diagonal: Three marks in a row on either of the two diagonal lines.
